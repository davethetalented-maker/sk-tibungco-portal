Who We Are

About SK Tibungco

The Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Tibungco is the official representative and governing body of the youth sector, mandated under RA 10742 to advance youth welfare, civic participation, and community development.

flag

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Barangay Tibungco through participatory governance, inclusive programs, and digital innovation — ensuring every young Tibungconon has a voice, access to services, and opportunities for growth.

lightbulb

Our Vision

A digitally-inclusive, transparent, and responsive barangay government that reflects the aspirations of its youth — a Tibungco where every young person thrives, leads, and belongs.

Legal Basis

Republic Act No. 10742

The Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Act of 2015 mandates the SK to formulate and implement programs and policies for the youth, prepare an Annual Budget and Program of Activities, and submit mandatory reports to ensure transparency and accountability in all transactions.
